Fustuk is an upcoming graphic novel written and illustrated by me about family, food, and cultural identity in a diaspora.
Set in a fantastical version of the Persian Empire, it follows the youngest of three dysfunctional siblings in a family of chefs who strike a deal with a Djinn to save their dying mother — but only if they can step out of the shadow of their legendary father and cook a dish to rival his.

Follow me on Twitter and Instagram (below) for progress, updates, and lots of art!
